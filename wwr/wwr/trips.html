Our Most Popular Trips

The Canyon Expedition

Embark on an unforgettable journey through the majestic Grand Canyon. This multi-day trip offers breathtaking views, exhilarating rapids, and unique camping experiences under the stars. Suitable for intermediate rafters looking for a truly immersive adventure.

Family Fun Float

Perfect for families with younger children or those seeking a more relaxed experience. This gentle float trip offers stunning scenery, opportunities for swimming, and plenty of wildlife viewing. A great introduction to the joys of river rafting!

Adrenaline Rush Rapid Run

For the thrill-seekers! This high-octane trip features Class IV and V rapids that will get your heart pumping. Previous rafting experience is recommended for this challenging and exciting adventure. Are you ready to conquer the rapids?
