Maui, Hawaii United States of America

Maui is the second-largest of the Hawaiian Islands and is known for its stunning beaches, lush landscapes, and vibrant culture. It is a popular tourist destination, offering activities such as snorkeling, hiking, and exploring the Haleakalā National Park. The island is also famous for its luxury resorts, including the Maui Marriott Hotel, which features a beautiful pool and ocean views. Maui's diverse ecosystems range from volcanic craters to tropical rainforests, making it a paradise for nature lovers.
